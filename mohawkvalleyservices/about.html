Our Mission

Mohawk Valley Services was built to connect residents across the Mohawk Valley with trusted local businesses. From plumbers in Fort Plain to electricians in Amsterdam, HVAC pros in Herkimer to well & septic services in Johnstown — we help you find the people who know your community.

The Mohawk Valley is a special place. Small towns, strong communities, and hardworking businesses that have served this region for generations. Our directory shines a light on the local pros who keep the valley running.

Why Local Matters

In a rural region like the Mohawk Valley, local business isn't just convenient — it's essential. The contractors, plumbers, and tech support folks in our directory live here too. They understand the old housing stock, the well water systems, the winter freeze-thaw cycles that stress every home from Fort Plain to Ilion.
