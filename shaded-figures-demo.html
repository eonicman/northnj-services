Replace Your Thermostat
● EASY ⏱️ 30 min 💰 $50-150

Upgrade to a programmable or smart thermostat to save 10-15% on heating/cooling bills.

⚠️ Safety First: Turn off power to your HVAC system at the breaker before starting. Verify with a non-contact voltage tester.
STEP 1
Turn off power at the breaker panel. Remove the old thermostat faceplate.
STEP 2
Label wires with the provided stickers. R, W, Y, G, C — before disconnecting.
STEP 3
Remove the old base by unscrewing from the wall. Keep screws if reusing.
STEP 4
Install new base — use a level to ensure it's straight. Drill new holes if needed.
STEP 5
Connect wires to matching terminals. Most modern thermostats have push-in connectors.
STEP 6
Attach faceplate and power on at the breaker. You should hear a click.
STEP 7
Configure settings — schedule, WiFi, preferences. Set your temperature schedule.
💡 Pro Tip: Take a photo of your old wiring before disconnecting — it's your safety net if you forget which wire goes where.
