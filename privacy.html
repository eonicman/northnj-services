Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Who We Are

North NJ Services is a local business directory operated by Eonic Systems, serving Northern New Jersey (Passaic, Bergen, Morris, and Sussex counties). Our website is at northnjservices.com.

Information We Collect

Visit counter: We use your browser's localStorage to count page visits. This data never leaves your device — it's just a number that increments each time you visit.

Meta Pixel: We use Meta (Facebook) Pixel to understand how people find our site. This places a cookie and sends anonymous visit data to Meta. See Meta's privacy policy.

Contact form: If you use our contact form, we store your message and email address to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share this data.

What We Don't Collect

We do not use Google Analytics, Google AdSense, or any third-party advertising networks. We do not sell or share personal data with data brokers. We do not track you across other websites.

Cookies

The Meta Pixel sets a first-party cookie (_fbp) for conversion tracking. Our visit counter uses localStorage (not a cookie) and is not shared with anyone.

Your Rights

You can clear localStorage at any time through your browser settings. You can opt out of Meta Pixel tracking using Meta's opt-out tools.
